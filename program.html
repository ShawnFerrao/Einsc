EINSC 2026 — Scientific Program

Education,
Simulation,
and Precision.

The EINSC 2026 Scientific Program is meticulously curated to transcend traditional lectures — featuring high-impact plenary sessions, provocative debates on surgical ethics, and specialized tracks in Basic Spinal and Cranial Surgery. From neuro-oncology to neuro-trauma management, our program offers a comprehensive roadmap for both seasoned consultants and early-career residents.
