Your EINSC 2026 Experience

Science, Surgery and Connection —
All Under One Roof

Plenary Hall & Keynote Lectures

The main auditorium hosts the opening ceremony, international keynote addresses, and plenary debates - bringing the full delegate body together for landmark sessions at the frontier of neurosurgery.

Surgical Skills Workshops

Dedicated hands-on workshop rooms run Basic Spinal and Cranial simulation sessions alongside the main programme - giving residents and early-career neurosurgeons direct access to practical training within the same venue.

Three Concurrent Session Rooms

Day two runs three simultaneous speciality tracks - Neuro-oncology, Spine & Trauma, and Functional Neurosurgery - allowing delegates to follow the stream most relevant to their practice without leaving the venue.

Industry Exhibition

A dedicated exhibition space where leading medical device and pharmaceutical partners present their latest innovations. Coffee breaks and refreshment intervals are scheduled to maximise your time on the exhibition floor.

Networking & Social Events

Structured networking sessions and evening social events - including a conference dinner - are hosted within Conrad Hotel Dubai's award-winning dining and event spaces, giving delegates the chance to build lasting professional relationships.

Stay On-Site, Miss Nothing

Delegates staying at Conrad Hotel Dubai are steps from every session, workshop, and event. The hotel's 248 rooms and suites offer panoramic city views, private balconies, and the signature Conrad service - so you arrive rested and focused.

Every Meal, a Networking
Opportunity

Conference lunches, coffee breaks and evening receptions take place across Conrad Dubai's dining venues - turning every interval into a chance to connect with colleagues. The conference gala dinner brings the full delegate body together in one of Dubai's most celebrated dining settings.

Ballaro
Sicilian · Live Cooking

Bustling live cooking and antipasti stations alongside a Sicilian a la carte menu created by Michelin-starred Chef Massimo Mantarro. A natural choice for conference dinners and faculty receptions.

Cave
Wine Bar · Modern Parisian

A chilled, intimate wine bar with top drops from the old and new world, innovative cocktails, and modern Parisian soul food - ideal for smaller faculty dinners and speaker gatherings.

Bliss 6
