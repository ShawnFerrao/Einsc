EINSC 2026 at Raffles Dubai

Two Days. Fourteen Sessions.
One Extraordinary Setting.

The 10th EINSC brings together neurosurgeons, residents, and healthcare professionals from across the region and beyond for two intensive days of scientific exchange. From opening keynotes to concurrent specialty sessions and hands-on surgical workshops - the full programme unfolds within Raffles Dubai's world-class conference facilities.

Delegates benefit from seamless access to every session, workshop, and networking event under one iconic roof, in a venue that combines five-star hospitality with the infrastructure to host a conference of this calibre.

The Conference Venue

Raffles Dubai

Rising above the iconic Wafi pyramid complex on Sheikh Rashid Road, Raffles Dubai is one of the most recognisable landmarks on the city skyline. As the host venue for EINSC 2026, it delivers world-class conference infrastructure alongside the legendary hospitality that has defined the Raffles name for over a century.

From its grand ballroom and purpose-built meeting rooms to its rooftop terrace and six award-winning restaurants, every detail has been considered - so delegates can focus entirely on the science, the sessions, and the connections that matter.

Your EINSC 2026 Experience

Science, Surgery and Connection —
All Under One Roof

Plenary Hall & Keynote Lectures

The main auditorium hosts the opening ceremony, international keynote addresses, and plenary debates - bringing the full delegate body together for landmark sessions at the frontier of neurosurgery.

Surgical Skills Workshops

Dedicated hands-on workshop rooms run Basic Spinal and Cranial simulation sessions alongside the main programme - giving residents and early-career neurosurgeons direct access to practical training within the same venue.

Three Concurrent Session Rooms

Day two runs three simultaneous speciality tracks - Neuro-oncology, Spine & Trauma, and Functional Neurosurgery - allowing delegates to follow the stream most relevant to their practice without leaving the venue.

Industry Exhibition

A dedicated exhibition space where leading medical device and pharmaceutical partners present their latest innovations. Coffee breaks and refreshment intervals are scheduled to maximise your time on the exhibition floor.

Networking & Social Events

Structured networking sessions and evening social events - including a conference dinner - are hosted within Raffles Dubai's award-winning dining and event spaces, giving delegates the chance to build lasting professional relationships.

Stay On-Site, Miss Nothing

Delegates staying at Raffles Dubai are steps from every session, workshop, and event. The hotel's 248 rooms and suites offer panoramic city views, private balconies, and the legendary Raffles service - so you arrive rested and focused.
