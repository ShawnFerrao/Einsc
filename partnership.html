Dear Esteemed Colleagues and Friends,

It is with great pride and pleasure that we welcome you to the 10th Edition of the Emirates International Neurosurgical Conference 2026 (EINSC), taking place from 16–18 October 2026 at the Conrad Hotel Dubai.

Marking a significant milestone, this 10th edition reflects a decade of scientific progress, collaboration, and shared commitment to advancing the field of neurosurgery. The conference brings together neurosurgeons, healthcare professionals, and experts from across the region and internationally for three days of insightful scientific exchange and professional engagement.

Your participation is integral to the success of this landmark edition. Together, we celebrate past achievements and look ahead to shaping the future of neurosurgery.

On behalf of the EINSC 2026 organizing committee, we look forward to welcoming you this October for an engaging and memorable 10th EINSC Conference.

Warm Regards, DR. KHALED FARES ALALI
About EINSC 2026

The Largest Neurosurgical
Conference in the GCC

The 10th Emirates International Neurosurgical Conference (EINSC) is the flagship event of the Emirates Society of Neurological Surgeons (ESNS), established in 2013 as a subspecialty of the Emirates Medical Association (EMA). EINSC has grown to become the largest neurosurgical conference in the GCC, serving as a premier platform for scientific exchange and professional development.

Each year, we bring together leading neurosurgeons, medical professionals, researchers, and healthcare companies from across the region and globally to discuss the latest advancements, techniques, and innovations in the field.
