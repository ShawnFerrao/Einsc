EINSC 2026 is proudly supported by leading neurosurgical societies from across the globe, lending their expertise, networks, and prestige to our 10th edition.

These organisations represent the collective expertise of thousands of neurosurgeons spanning South Asia, Southeast Asia, the Pacific, and the Arab world. Their endorsement reflects the truly international character of EINSC 2026 — a conference that brings the world’s neurosurgical community to Dubai.
