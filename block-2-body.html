About EINSC 2026

The largest Neurosurgical Conference in the GCC

Welcome to the 10th Emirates Society of Neurological Surgeons (ESNS) Conference, the region's premier forum for neurosurgical advancement. Taking place from 16–18 October 2026 at the iconic Conrad Hotel Dubai, this landmark decennial edition marks ten years of unwavering commitment to scientific progress, clinical excellence, and global collaboration.

The 10th ESNS Conference is uniquely designed to serve the entire neurosurgical community. Our program facilitates a high-level exchange between world-renowned experts and the next generation of neurosurgeons.

A Message from the Organising Committee

Welcome to the 10th EINSC

“It is with great pride and pleasure that we welcome you to the 10th Emirates Society of Neurological Surgeons (ESNS) Conference, set to take place from 16–18 October 2026 at the Conrad Hotel Dubai.”

Marking a significant milestone, this 10th edition reflects a decade of scientific progress, collaboration, and shared commitment to advancing the field of neurosurgery. The conference brings together neurosurgeons, healthcare professionals, and experts from across the region and internationally for three days of insightful scientific exchange and professional engagement.

The programme has been thoughtfully curated to showcase the latest developments, innovative techniques, and emerging trends in neurosurgery, while providing a platform for meaningful discussion and knowledge sharing among peers.

In addition to the scientific sessions, the conference offers valuable networking opportunities designed to strengthen professional relationships and foster future collaboration within our growing neurosurgical community.

Your participation is integral to the success of this landmark edition. Together, we celebrate past achievements and look ahead to shaping the future of neurosurgery.

On behalf of the EINSC 2026 Organising Committee
